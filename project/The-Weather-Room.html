The Weather Room

An interactive installation, a collaborative conceptual project, interactive coding, p5.js

The Weather Room is an interactive installation, created in collaboration with Kilian Hoppner. It is a dynamic installation where viewers influence the ambience of a room with their presence. Projected light changes the colour from dark blue tones to warm orange hues based on the sound frequencies in the room. There are recorded clips of peopleâ€™s interactions in public spaces playing in the background, creating a visual representation of the collective human experience with the environment. This fosters a deep reflection of our relationship with nature.
