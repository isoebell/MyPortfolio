Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

Printed book, a typographic exploration

Exhibited at the 2024 Melbourne Art Book Fair

This concrete poetry approach to typesetting ‘Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland,’ invites the reader to engage with the story in a way that’s atypical to its traditional reading experience; exploring how the typographic forms influences the way that we read.

This book was part of the Open|Close Exhibition held as part of the 2024 Melbourne Art Book Fair.
