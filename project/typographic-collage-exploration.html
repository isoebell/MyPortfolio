Typographic collage experiments

Using collage techniques, found materials, tags, transport tickets, and stamps

Some playful typographic experiments. Using collage techniques and found materials to push these type relationships. From conventional, to unconventional, until something interesting happens. An analogue exercise that warms up your creativity, and forces you to think differently.

These A5 prints were sold at a fundraiser with all profits donated to Palestinian Children's Relief Fund (PCRF), the remainder were sold with 100% of donations going towards mutual aid for Palestinian families in need in Melbourne.
