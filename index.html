I’m a graphic designer and ceramicist based in Naarm (Melbourne). My interests span many, however notably I enjoy the world of the digital; be it creative coding, web design, web development, or 3D art. That being said, I do also hold a special place in my heart for typographic and publication design.

I acknowledge the Boonwurrung, Woi Wurrung and Wurundjeri peoples of the Kulin Nation as the traditional custodians of the unceded lands on which I live, work and create. Sovereignty was never ceded.
